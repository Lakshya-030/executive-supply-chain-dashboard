# Executive Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel. The dashboard helps analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and delivery performance through dynamic KPIs, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.

The dashboard enables users to explore business performance by filtering data based on Market, Customer Segment, and Shipping Mode.

## Business Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance
- Track sales, profit, and order volume
- Analyze profit margin across different filters
- Evaluate delivery performance
- Identify high-performing product categories
- Compare sales across different markets and customer segments

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Profit Margin (%)
- On-Time Delivery (%)
- Average Delivery Variance (Days)

---

## Dashboard Features

- Dynamic KPI Cards
- Interactive Slicers
- Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- Sales by Market
- Customer Segment Distribution
- Top 10 Product Categories by Profit
- Automatic KPI updates using Pivot Tables

## Key Business Insights

- Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales.
- Sales performance varies significantly across different markets.
- A small number of product categories contribute a large portion of total profit.
- Delivery performance changes based on shipping mode and market.
- Interactive slicers enable detailed business analysis across multiple dimensions.
